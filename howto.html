How To navigate and complete The Quiz!


Instructions:

1. Click on the 'Do The Quiz!' link on the menu bar above to go to the quiz page.
2. Click on 'Start Quiz!' to start the quiz and the TIMER.
3. Answer the 10 questions (multiple choice) to see your score.
4. Keep an eye on the Timer as this influences your end score!
5. You ONLY have 10 seconds to answer each question!!.
6. Brag to everybody how good you are at Quizes...!!!
